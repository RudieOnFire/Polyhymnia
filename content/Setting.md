The world of polyhymnia is a modern world that the greek mythology pantheon has kept within its grip. As the ages went by, nations have risen and fallen, the sprawling cities have crumbled and gaia has reclaimed much of her land. Cities still remain and is where much of the populous live, though there are modest villages and towns still scattered throughout. 

There are modern technologies handmade by a small cohort called the Hephaesti Guild. The Hephaesti Guild, guided by the ethos of hephaestus, are intelligent tinkerers, inventors, constuctors, and engineers who together have created innovations ahead of their time considering the population of polyhymnia. Small cars and trucks are used, though they are rare since the mass production era was generations ago. Clean energy is used, efficiencies were researched ages ago to deliver super efficient solar and wind power. The engineering and construction of these were done long ago but they have been impeccably maintained. The aether net is an early form of internet where people can share information from centralized computer hubs or aether net cafes. They are not usually seen in household because of the cost. People carry Styloses, which are early cell phones, which are used for communication and note taking but do not connect to the aether net. Most technologies are confined to the cities, as that's where the money is. 

The land is sprawling with green and mountains and is idyllic.
